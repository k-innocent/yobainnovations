Admissions Process

Welcome to the admissions page of our institution. Here you will find all the information you need to apply.

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Official transcripts
  • Personal statement
  • Letters of recommendation

Important Dates

Make sure to keep track of the following dates:

  • Application Deadline: June 1, 2023
  • Interviews: June 15-30, 2023
  • Decision Notification: July 15, 2023

How to Apply

To apply, please follow these steps:

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Submit your documents as specified above.
  3. Prepare for an interview if selected.
